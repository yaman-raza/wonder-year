The Problem
The Premium Trap
India's baby gear market has a costly, cluttered problem every parent knows too well.
01
💸
High Upfront Costs
Parents shell out lakhs on premium brands like Chicco and LuvLap — gear that costs more than a month's rent.
02
⏱️
Short Lifecycles
Most expensive baby items are outgrown within 3–6 months, making them the world's priciest temporary purchases.
03
📦
The Clutter Crisis
Strollers, cribs, rockers — they pile up as "storage wasters" or get sold for a fraction of what you paid.
The Solution
Subscribe, Swap & Grow
Our Subscribe & Swap model adapts to your child's milestones — so you always have the right gear at the right time.
1
Choose Your Plan
Pick a single item rental or a Milestone Bundle tailored to your baby's age and stage. Fully customisable.
2
We Deliver, Spotless
Every item undergoes Deep UV-C sterilization before door-to-door delivery — cleaner than fresh out of the box.
3
Swap as They Grow
Track your child's milestones in our app. When it's time for the next stage, we swap the gear — you do nothing.
4
We Pick It Up
When done, we collect everything. No selling, no storing, no stress. Your home stays clutter-free.
